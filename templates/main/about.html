About Doclumina

From Advocacy to Action — Our Journey to Transforming Healthcare Access in Nigeria.

What began as a grassroots support movement for house officers, guiding them through housemanship in Nigeria and Post house Job opportunities—TheyHaveLeftYouBehind—has grown into a powerful, purpose-driven health-tech platform now known as Doclumina.

500+ Doctors Supported
200+ Hospitals Connected
Our Mission

How It All Started

Launched in response to the frustrations and delays young Nigerian doctors face in securing housemanship placements, TheyHaveLeftYouBehind quickly became a beacon of hope. With over 5,000 medical graduates supported, it proved that when doctors help doctors, lives change.

But we didn't stop there. We listened to the deeper pain points—the gaps in patient access to timely expert care, the mental strain on frontline workers, and the professional stagnation many young doctors face post-NYSC. That’s how Doclumina was born.

Our Vision

Transforming Healthcare Through Technology

To become the leading digital platform that empowers African medical professionals, fostering excellence in healthcare delivery through innovative technology solutions, comprehensive mentorship programs, and strategic partnerships across the continent.
