Housemanship FAQs

Essential information about housemanship in Nigeria

Can I get housemanship without being inducted?

NO. Induction is mandatory before you can start housemanship.

Can I create account in the housemanship portal without having provisional license approved?

NO. Your provisional license must be approved before you can create an account on the portal.

How often does the housemanship portal get updated with new centers?

This occurs every 2 months.

Should I start checking the portal at midnight when centers are uploaded?

NO. Start in the morning, say 9am, except otherwise stated.

Can I start house job in a center and later transfer to another center to continue?

NO, not possible. However, you can resign from one center and start afresh in another center.

Am I allowed to get multiple centers at the same time?

NO. You can only be assigned to one center at a time.

Can I move from a portal center to a non-portal center like State, private or Military hospital?

Yes you can.

How long before you get your first salary?

It takes about 2 months for portal centers.

I have not been paid salary since I started work, what can I do?

Send a mail to MDCN at houseofficersfinance@mdcn.gov.ng

I wish to resign from my house job center due to personal reasons, how can I?

Send a mail to MDCN at housemanship@mdcn.gov.ng

If I resign from a portal center, am I expected to refund MDCN the salaries I received?

YES. You must refund MDCN the salaries received before you can get another portal center.

Which hospital pays the highest salary for House Officers?

HSC Lagos pays ₦374,000 monthly.

Can I get my full registration without doing house job?

No, you can't. House job is mandatory for full registration.

What is the fastest I can finish my house job?

11 months is the fastest duration possible.

How can I get difficult centers like Abuja?

Through the portal and housemanship networking.

What are the most sought-after centers in Nigeria?

NHA, FMC Jabi, UATH, HSC Lagos, LASUTH, FMC Ebute Metta, FMC Asaba, and HMB Abuja.
