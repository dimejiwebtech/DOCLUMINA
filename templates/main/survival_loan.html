Survival Loan for House Officers

This loan is specifically tailored for Nigerian House Officers, recognizing the frequent delays in their salaries—hence the name "Survival Loan."

You become eligible for this loan once you have completed at least two months of your housemanship and have no more than two months remaining before completion. To qualify, you must be currently serving in a hospital listed under the MDCN portal.
